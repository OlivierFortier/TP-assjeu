This pack is ideal for creating your own village. 
Materials contain diffuse map, normal map, height map and occlusion map in high quality. 
Resolution are 512x512, 1024x1024, 2048x2048 it depends on object. 
The collection includes: 13 building prefabs, 8 props prefabs. 
Several tiled textures (bricks and wood).
Packege include only 3D models and textures.

Prefabs:
- Barrel
- Boat
- Cabin
- Gallows
- Molo
- Pail
- Shelter
- Table 
- Wagon
- Well
- Lamp
- Pile of wood
- House prefabs with wood in the window and with glass in window (only first floor). 

In FBX folder are original 3D models.
